# Coffee-Shop-Sales
Created an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze shop trends. Performed data cleaning and calculations in Excel to generate additional insights that support chart visualizations and key business questions.

## Questions and Answers
##### 1. What are the top 5 best-selling coffee products based on quantity ordered? <br>
  ###### Answer: ProductId(A-D-0.2, A-M-0.5, A-M-1, A-M-2.5, R-L-0.2)
#### 2. Which coffee type has the highest total revenue? <br>
   Answer: Excelsa
#### 3. Which product size sells the most? <br>
   Answer: Tall
#### 4. Which coffee type is the most profitable overall? <br>
   Answer: Liberica
#### 5. Which roast type generates the highest total profit? <br>
   Answer: Light
#### 6. Are higher priced coffees selling less or more? <br>
   Answer: Higher-priced coffees sell less.
#### 7. Is there a relationship between price and sales volume? <br>
   Answer: Yes, because sales volume decreases when the price is higher.
#### 8. Which products have high price but low sales (quantity)? <br> 
   Answer: ProductId(E-L-2.5, L-L-2.5)
#### 9. Which products have low price but high sales (quantity)? <br>
   Answer: ProductId R-L-0.2
#### 10. Which month has the highest sales? <br>
   Answer: June
#### 11. Which roast level do most customers prefer? <br>
   Answer: Medium Roast Type
#### 12. Do customers prefer smaller or larger coffee sizes? <br>
   Answer: They prefer tall and short coffee cup
#### 13. Which coffee products should the company promote more? <br>
   Answer: ProductId L-D-2.5 because it generates highest profit
#### 14. What coffee type should the business focus on to maximize profit? <br>
   Answer: Liberica
